# 01 — Personas & jobs-to-be-done

DuoData serves Amanoters. Personas below cover the audience targeted by the MVP. Job titles use Amanotes job grades (P1–E).

## Primary personas

### P1 — New Product Owner ("Linh, P1 PO on PIa")

- **Job**: figure out which product knobs to turn first to move PIa's UV7 + R7.
- **Context**: 3 weeks in. Has read the metric tree Confluence page twice and still cannot recite the Act-date vs Cohort difference under pressure.
- **JTBD**: *"When I look at a daily Metabase dashboard, help me know which numbers are act-date and which are cohort, what drives each, and where my levers are."*
- **Wins when**: She finishes Product 101 + Mon 101 + Cross-Functional Connections in week 4 and can lead her first weekly product review.

### P2 — UA Specialist ("Khoa, P2 UA on LO4 portfolio")

- **Job**: bid efficiently on Google / TikTok / IronSource for BHi, GDUCa, PIi without burning budget.
- **Context**: Knows CPI / ROAS by heart but is hazy on UV → ROAS math, eROAS vs Paid ROAS, and how Creative IPM feeds his numbers.
- **JTBD**: *"When I see a creative's ROAS D7 spike, help me know whether it's a paid-quality signal, an organic-uplift artifact, or a network-mix shift."*
- **Wins when**: He finishes UA 101 + Cross-Functional Connections and can explain the eROAS caveat to his lead without checking notes.

### P2 — Monetization Operator ("Mai, P2 MO on PI")

- **Job**: optimize ad placement (RW / FS / BN / AOA) and IAP funnel (Starter Pack, No Ads, Piggy Bank) to lift ARPDAU + UV.
- **Context**: Strong on ad ops, weak on the UA-side picture (CPI, ROAS, organic uplift) — she ships ad-density changes that lift ARPDAU but tank R1, which UA only catches a week later.
- **JTBD**: *"Help me see the second-order effect of an ad-density change on retention and on UA's ROAS — before I ship it."*
- **Wins when**: She finishes Mon 101 + Cross-Functional Connections and starts pre-flagging UA on monetization experiments.

### P3 — Senior Game Designer ("Tuan, P3 GD on GDUC")

- **Job**: pitch new mechanics with a credible metric impact claim.
- **Context**: Comfortable with R1/R7, sumME, FTUE completion. Avoids monetization numbers; treats UA as "someone else's problem".
- **JTBD**: *"Help me phrase a feature hypothesis in the standard format and tie it to the metric tree, so my pitches survive Product review."*
- **Wins when**: He completes Cross-Functional Connections and uses the canonical hypothesis format in 2 consecutive pitch decks.

### M2 — Manager ("Phuong, M2 Product Manager, LiveOps")

- **Job**: align her squad on cycle goals, run weekly reviews, escalate to M3/M4.
- **Context**: Knows the metric tree but spends ~3 hrs/week unblocking junior staff on basic metric questions.
- **JTBD**: *"Cut the time I spend re-explaining UV vs LTV vs ARPDAU to new hires."*
- **Wins when**: New hires arrive having completed onboarding paths; her time on metric tutoring drops.

## Secondary personas (not primary MVP audience, but considered)

- **Data Analyst (P2/P3)** — already metric-fluent; uses DuoData to teach others, may also write content.
- **Music Specialist (P2)** — needs Product-side basics to discuss song performance with PO and UA.
- **Leadership (M3/M4/E)** — uses path-completion dashboard as a clarity signal for their function.

## Anti-personas

- Job applicants and partners. DuoData is internal-only; do not expose content publicly.
- Non-metric learning needs (e.g., learning Unity, Music theory). Out of scope.
